The Founder

From a blank canvas
to 50+ brands built.

I'm Abdelrahman Amari — Art Director, food photographer, and the person behind Yalla Moment. My story started at 10 years old, not watching cartoons, but glued to design shows that lit a fire I've never been able to put out.

That passion pushed me to master graphic design, food photography, and digital marketing — all in service of one goal: helping businesses in Egypt stand out. I've worked with over 100 restaurants and built this agency from scratch.

"I learned from people whose only goal was to share knowledge freely. That's the same standard I hold myself to."

The Team

In-house experts,
one unified vision.

Yalla Moment isn't a one-man show. We're a tight-knit team of designers, photographers, videographers, developers, and strategists who believe great marketing is always a team sport.
